The Best Movies According to Josie

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Insidious 2

A great sequel to the first movie. I love they way they interwined the second movie into the first movie. You would need to watch the first to understand and enjoy the creative film. Highly reccomend!

White Chicks

This is an old favorite of mine. Reccomend if you like a good comedy.

Black Panther: Wakonda Forever

If you like Marvel, action and a good story plot... Look no further.
